============================================================
 FRONT MOTOR (GPIO 4)
============================================================

ESP32‑C3 GPIO 4 ----100Ω----> Base (B)
                             |
Motor -  ------------------> Collector (C)
Motor +  -----> +5V
                             |
Emitter (E) ---------------> GND

Diode: 1N4148 or 1N4007 across motor
Stripe end → +5V
Non‑stripe → Motor -
